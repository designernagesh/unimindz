UniMindz was started with a vision to provide business and human resource consulting solutions across industries especially in IT and Non-IT sectors (Education, Hospitality, FMCG, Telecom, Media and Entertainment). We specialize in selection systems for jobs that require unusual combination of skills, motivational factors and other personal characteristics that give our clients a competitive advantage.

Our Belief:

As talent providers, we always believe that our success is predicated upon our clients’ success. We understand that workforce and systems are critical to success. So we work hard to understand your organization’s unique needs and culture to deliver the best answers to your talent acquisition and system development challenges. Our growth has been largely through referrals from our existing clients and various professionals.

Our Mission:

We always believe in consistent and continuous improvements. In our constant endeavour to become the preferred Placement services company, we are continuously updating our candidate databases and our selection methodologies.

Challenges:

Today’s business challenges require ongoing business transformation to respond to the rapidly changing market conditions with innovation and agility. They present a new wave of HR, talent and organization priorities. Organizations struggle to find the right talent to keep pace with business demands.

Our Team :

Our management team and consultant team consists of individuals from both IT and Non-IT background. Team’s average experience is more than 10 years and had rich work experience in top national and international organizations of high repute. We are supported by active advisors who are veterans and have rich experience in IT, Education, Engineering, Training, Healthcare, Hotel Management, Designing, Quality Assurance, Media and Entertainment. Our well experienced quality assurance team takes care of our internal process and also helps clients in setting up the quality process and managing it as per the requirements. We help you in creating a people strategy that supports your overall business goals.
